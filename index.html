Overview and Mission Statement

Overview

Likhanyile Consulting Engineers and Project Managers is committed to fostering close working relationships with clients in through a good understanding of their project requirements at all times. The company draws from a host of highly skilled and experienced individuals, both internally and through partnerships and alliance agreements to render quality services within the requisite time frame cost and quality. The company will enter into joint venture agreements with larger firms to implement large projects that it may not have the capacity to handle on its own. This will be done with the purpose of skills transfer and increasing capacity within the practice.

Mission Statement

Likhanyile Consulting Engineers and Project Managers is committed to supporting initiatives and interventions that seek to redress social and economic imbalances.

About

Quality Assurance

Likhanyile Consulting Engineers and Project Managers has developed a comprehensive Quality Assurance Policy Manual and associated Procedures Manual along the guidelines proposed by SABS ISO 9001. The system addresses all the activities of the various stages of engineering projects.

Additionally, the company’s Site Management Manual, specifically shows our commitment to ensuring that an effective Quality System is applied during the Construction Monitoring phase.

Health Safety Policy

Likhanyile Consulting Engineers and Project Managers is committed to ensuring that projects are implemented with compliance with the Occupational Health and Safety Act and its construction regulations. To issue written instructions to the contractors and their sub-contractors involved in any operations and to implement the necessary safety measures to ensure that the lives and health of the public is not endangered.
In its pursuit of quality in all aspects of designing and monitoring, Likhanyile Consulting Engineers and Project Managers recognizes the significance of integrating health and safety into all construction staff. Through its management structure and with the support of all staff, employer, contractor and sub-contractors, Likhanyile Consulting Engineers and Project Managers will provide and maintain the highest possible standards of health, safety and well-being for all staff while they are at work.
Likhanyile Consulting Engineers and Project Managers will provide a consultancy service of Occupational Health and Safety to the construction staff and to oversee the implementation of the contractor’s health and safety management plan.
